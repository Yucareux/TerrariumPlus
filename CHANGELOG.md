# TerrariumPlus Changelog

## TerrariumPlus Alpha Build
- Snow cover strictly follows ESA land-cover classifications.
- Sea level setting now supports Automatic (default) while staying aligned with height offset.
- Manual sea level overrides adjust ocean waterline without shifting overall terrain.
- Inland lakes and rivers stay at ESA-derived elevations even with custom sea level.
- Sea level defaults updated to 64 in presets.
- ESA water/no-data biomes consult the water resolver before assigning ocean or river.
